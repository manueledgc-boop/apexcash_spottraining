SIMILARITY SERVICE
=====================================================

Preparado para Spot Family.

Regla objetivo:

PRE-FLOP

Debe coincidir:

- Street
- Hero Position
- Villain Position
- Spot Family

Si Spot Family no coincide:

Score = 0
No Match

Esto evita respuestas incorrectas.
